Summary: Latitude vs X



Given the very flat nature of the plot, it seems reasonable to assume that wind speed and latitude are not strongly correlated at all. There are some outliers at the higher latitudes, and perhaps a larger sampling of the extremes would reveal a tendency at higher latitudes to have higher wind speeds, but as our data stands we cannot make any definitive conclusions as to a correlation being present.
